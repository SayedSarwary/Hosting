Dr. Iffat Sabir Chaudhry

Dr. Iffat Sabir Chaudhry is an Associate Professor of Human Resource Management and Organizational Behavior at Al Ain University, UAE. With over a decade of academic experience across three countries, she has led curriculum reforms, accreditation initiatives, and programs integrating sustainability and inclusive leadership into business education. Her research bridges theory and practice, focusing on leadership, organizational behavior, AI in HR, and responsible management, with publications in high-impact journals. Through mentoring, community engagement, and industry collaborations, she cultivates socially conscious leaders and contributes meaningfully to both academic and societal development. She is committed to her core values of:

Impact – driving meaningful change.

Sustainability – championing responsibility and stewardship.

Compassion – caring for and supporting others.
